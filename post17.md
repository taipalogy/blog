## VI. Hard Constraints

* The object must **never act autonomously**; it cannot initiate motion, force, or effects without the figure’s action.
* The figure never interacts with the screens.
* The object may be **rescaled only in the Main Portrait** to enable dynamic interaction while maintaining physics.

## VII. Linguistic Action Enforcement

**Figure-Initiated Gerund Rule:**

When an action in Slot I includes a **gerund or present participle** (-ing form), the figure must be the **explicit actor and initiator** of that action.

**Interpretation Rule:**

If the phrasing implies motion or state (e.g., gliding, shaping, bending, tearing), the image must show **how the figure physically causes and controls** that action through contact with the object.
