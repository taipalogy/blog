````
**Input**: *"The combination of a pencil and imagination resulted in a story."*

1. **Tree-Based Parsing**:
```
ROOT: resulted  
  ├── SUBJECT: combination  
  │   ├── MODIFIER: the

```

- *a*: det (determiner of *story*)  
- *in*: case (preposition)
```
````
